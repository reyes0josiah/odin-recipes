Chicken Tinola

Chicken Tinola with Moringga Oleifera Leaves

Chicken Tinola is cooked by boiling Chicken along with other flavors provided with seasonings and additions.

A Filipino Favorite, not only does it taste good it's pretty healthy too! A balance of Protein from the meat and various Vitamins from the Superfood Moringga Oleifera Leaves! A Fulling meal for you.

  • 0.5 kg --- Chicken Leg
  • 2 sachets --- Magic Sarap
  • 2 tbsp --- Palm Oil
  • 4 cloves --- Garlic
  • 1 pc --- Ginger
  • 1 pc --- Onion
  • 1 stalk --- Lemon Grass
  • 1 pc --- Finger Chili
  • 2 cups --- Papaya/Sayote
  1. Season chicken with 1 sachet magic sarap. Set aside.
  2. Saut√© garlic, ginger, onion, lemongrass (optional) and finger chili in oil. Add chicken and cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Pour water, boil, skim the scum and simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Add papaya and simmer for another 10 minutes.
  5. Season with remaining 1 sachet of magic sarap. Stir in chili tops. Transfer into a serving bowl and serve hot.
